About Us

Fishers of Faith is an organization within the St. Rose Family of Parishes in Springfield, Ohio. A group formed out of the Exodus 90 2025 year that aims to bring people back to the Faith and back to the mass. An oppurtunity to bring the St. Rose Family of Parishes together as a community and to welcome God into our lives so as to make an impact on each one of us.

History

Where We Started?

Fishers of Faith originally started around the end of the 90 day Exodus 90 journey. Near the end of Exodus 90, we spoke as a fraternity the importance of going out and trying to encourage people to come back to the Church. Thie 2025 year, we've found that there is a plenty of new things occuring whether its a new pope, archbishop, priests, etc., we felt that now is a time of rebirth and an oppurtunity to bring people back to the faith. That's why this group was formed.

Info On Exodus 90

Exodus 90 is an organization that helps your Church build a fraternity of men centered around the concept of focus, accountabilitiy, and personal growth specifically for men. It has main 12 features with many more but primarily focues on these: Fasting & Feasting, Monthly Challenges, Scripture Passage & Reflection, Spiritual Guides, Prayer Experiences, Personal Plan of Life, Fraternity Meeting Guide, Check-In, Group Chats & DMs, Journals, Local Communities, and Remote Fraternities.

These 12 features are contained withing different seasons of the year. These seasons are Exodus 90, Easter 50: Entering The Promised Land, Kings of Summer: FIghting Sloth and Pride, St. Michael's Lent: The World Unveiled, Fall Challenge: Men of Wisdom and Peace, Month of the Dead: Memento Mori, Advent Challenge: God's Loving Presence, and Christmas Season: Answer The Call

Goals

The goal of Fisher's of Faith is to bring back as many people as possible to the Church and specifically the mass. There's been a large numebr of people who have dropped out of coming to the mass. Through hosting Church events in the Springfield, Ohio area, we hope to make a change to bring people back.
